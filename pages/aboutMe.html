About Me

Hi, my name is Przemek. I started writing this blog to share my thoughts on different topics and share my groundhopping experiences, which I gather all over the world.

Football

Footbal became my passion already when I was a kid and now it is my biggest life passion. I love watching games live, especially the ones from the lowest levels.
My favourite leagues are: Polish B-Klasa, English leagues, which belong to English Football League and Swiss 1., 2., 3., 4. and 5. Liga.

Groundhopping

Football is my biggest life passion, but not the only one. I love watching basketball, tennis and other sports too.
In groundhopping section I will be sharing my groundhopping experiences from other sports.

Legia Warszawa

Legia Warszawa is my favourite club. I became its supporter when I was a kid and I still cannot miss its single game.
In this section I will be sharing my thoughts on club's recent activity.
